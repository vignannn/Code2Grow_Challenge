The smart canteen billing system is a c program used to calculate bills in a college canteen . It asks how many customers are there for the day . For each customer , it asks how many items they want to order for every item , it takes the item code and quantity it checks the item code and finds the correct price for that item it multiplies the price and quantity to find the total for that item if the total bill is more than 500 it gives a ten percent discount it then shows the total amount discount and final bill for the customer the final bill of each customer is added to the total revenue of the canteen after all customers are served it shows the total number of customers and the total revenue earned that day it uses loops and conditions to repeat the billing steps and make the process automatic .
